12th Ave. Square Park

About 12th Ave. Square Park

12th Ave. Square park is a park located at 564 12th Ave, Seattle, WA 98122. This park not only offers a great place to sit and hang out, it has great places to eat like Cherry Street Coffee, and BaBar. This park is also Child friendly as it has a giant quishy dome to play on.

The park is open from 4AM to 11:30PM

Ba Bar

"This is one of my favorite happy hour spots. I've never had a problem getting a bar table and I've found the staff super friendly. I think their happy hour food deals are some of the best around. You can get pho for $8 and a number of other really great snacks for the same amount of less. One of my favorites is the tofu and cabbage salad, which doesn't sound exciting, but once you try the dressing and the crispy shallots on it, you will be hooked! I think their portions are pretty generous, too so two people can feel satisfied with only a few items."

"Love this spot, especially for those REALLY LATE night eats. Tons of vegetarian options and always great, fresh ingredients. Service is a bit spotty at times but worth it. They're almost always packed which is of course a good sign. Reasonably priced and I really enjoy the darker mellow ambiance. They can accommodate groups but it's nice to just go with one other person. Food comes out relatively quickly depending on how busy they are."

"Can't wrap my head around the low Yelp reviews. We have had a great time when we stopped in for drinks and dinner. Everything we ordered was tasty! I highly recommend the Xiu Mai...they are fabulous meatballs in a sauce with bread and the pho ga is good too. We will be back soon."
